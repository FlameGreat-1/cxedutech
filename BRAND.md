# 🧠 2. COLOR STRATEGY (THIS IS THE SECRET)

This brand is built on:

### 👉 “Controlled Chaos”

Lots of colors BUT:

* Only **1–2 dominant per section**
* Others act as **accents**

### Rule:

> 70% neutral + 20% primary + 10% accent

If you ignore this → your UI will look messy.

# 🧩 7. HOW TO APPLY THIS TO YOUR DASHBOARD

Here’s a **real structure you can follow**:

## Sidebar

* Dark green or neutral
* Active item = bright green

## Topbar

* White or light
* Minimal

## Cards

* Mostly white
* Occasionally colored (green/blue sections)

## Buttons

* Primary → Green
* Secondary → Blue
* CTA → Orange

## Charts

* Use:

  * Green (positive)
  * Blue (neutral)
  * Orange (alerts)

## Background

* Mostly white
* Add subtle abstract patterns in sections

* Keep structure clean
* Add color intentionally
