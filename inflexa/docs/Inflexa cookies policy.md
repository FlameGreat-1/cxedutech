## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website.

They help websites to:

- Function properly
- Remember preferences
- Improve user experience
- Analyse performance

Cookies may be "session cookies" (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (stored for longer).
