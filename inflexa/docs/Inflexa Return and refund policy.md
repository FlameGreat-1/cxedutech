# Returns & Refunds Policy
## Inflexa Technologies Limited

**Last Updated: 15/2/2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

This Returns & Refunds Policy explains how customers may return products and request refunds from Inflexa Technologies Limited ("Inflexa", "we", "us", "our").

By placing an order, you agree to this policy.

This policy applies to all purchases made through our website.

---

## 2. Eligibility for Returns

You may be eligible for a return or refund if:

- You received the wrong item
- Your item arrived damaged or defective
- Your order is incomplete
- You cancel within the legal cooling-off period

Returns must meet the conditions outlined below.

---

## 3. Cooling-Off Period (Right to Cancel)

If you are a consumer in the UK, EU, or other jurisdictions with similar laws, you have the right to cancel your order within:

**14 days from the date you receive your goods, without providing a reason.**

To exercise this right, you must notify us in writing within 14 days.

---

## 4. Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return:

- Items must be unused
- Items must be in original packaging
- All materials must be included
- Items must be in resalable condition

We reserve the right to refuse returns that do not meet these conditions.

---

## 5. How to Request a Return

To initiate a return:

1. Email us at: inflexatechnologies@gmail.com
2. Include:
   - Order number
   - Reason for return
   - Photos (if damaged/defective)
3. We will respond within 3–5 business days with return instructions.

Do not send items back without approval.

---

## 6. Return Shipping

### A. Faulty or Incorrect Items

If the return is due to our error, we will cover return shipping costs.

### B. Change of Mind

If returning due to preference or cancellation, you are responsible for return shipping.

We recommend using a tracked service.

---

## 7. Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item:

- We will notify you of approval or rejection
- Approved refunds are processed within 10 business days
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method

Processing times may vary by payment provider.

---

## 8. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned unless faulty:

- Downloadable digital products
- Opened or heavily used materials
- Customised products
- Clearance items (where stated)

---

## 9. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges.

Please return the item and place a new order.

---

## 10. Damaged in Transit

If your order arrives damaged:

- Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
- Provide photos of damage and packaging

We will arrange replacement or refund.

---

## 11. International Orders

For international customers:

- You are responsible for customs duties and taxes
- Original shipping fees are non-refundable (unless faulty)
- Return shipping may be higher due to location

We are not responsible for delays caused by customs.

---

## 12. Chargebacks & Disputes

If you have an issue with your order, please contact us first.

Filing a chargeback without contacting us may delay resolution and affect future orders.

We aim to resolve disputes fairly and promptly.

---

This policy shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of England and Wales.

If you are a consumer residing outside the United Kingdom, you may also benefit from any mandatory provisions of the consumer protection laws of your country of residence.

Nothing in this policy affects your rights under such mandatory local laws.

Subject to the above, the courts of England and Wales shall have exclusive jurisdiction.
